This is a bakery-themed clone of Lumosity's Pinball Recall game, and guess what? You're the Dough-Boy. It tests your memory of a constellation of bumpers by asking you to predict where your Dough-Boy icon will have to start in order to end up at the baguette. Neither the hero nor the target are visible yet, and you will only see the bumper pattern for ca. 5 seconds.

Your movements are determined by the positions of the bumpers, and once you're out of your starting gate, you no longer have control over your Dough-Boy. However, every endpoint is connected to a unique origin point, so only one starting point will be the right answer!

Higher levels mean more bumpers and, therefore, more complex routes to memorize.

The easiest way to get the hang of it is to play. So have fun!
